Product Features

Let your home welcome you

Set mood lighting for your house and enjoy the ambience as per you mood. You can dim your lights, turn on your AC and play your favorite music on your way back home and have your home welcome you. You can set mood lighting for party at home or just a quite dinner with your family.

Monitor your electricity consumption without paying extra

Do you sometimes wonder why your electricity bills were so high when you were not even there at home? Automate your appliances and electrical fittings to keep tabs on your house and curb those expensive bills. The smart plug controls your appliances like AC, geyser and analyzes energy usage for the day, week or a month. At any point in time, you can check whether your appliance is on or off.

An unparalleled experience

With ‘Curtain controller’, schedule your curtains to open, by itself and wake you up in the morning and close at mid-day when you are out for work. ‘Smart door lock’ allows keyless access to people at your command. You don’t need to worry about always carrying your keys or giving a copy of your house key to the helpers, hence, making your home a safer place.

Specially designed for Indian environment

We provide completely modular solutions that can fit in all Indian homes, old or new. With a range of easy to install products and the ability to create customizable scenarios, our solutions are answer for all your home/office automation requirements. All you need is a smartphone, a wifi connection and our best in-class products to simplify the mundane tasks of managing your home.

Our Offerings

Products

Plug

Simple, convenient and versatile. Smart plug helps you control and monitor heavy loads like that of air-conditioners and geysers from your phone

Motion Sensor

Motion Sensor is a battery based sensor that works on RF Technology to help you enable motion detection and prevent any intrusions for enhanced security

Door Sensor

Switches on your lights when you open the door. Enhance security of your house by receiving alerts if a door or window is opened.

Smart Door lock

Give access to people and not your keys. Grant key-less access to family, friends and housekeepers at your command and not worry about losing your keys anymore. Make your home a safer place.

Pert 4 Node

The Pert 4-Node fits into your existing switchboard, and allows you to control and monitor physical switches in your home.

Pert 8 Node

The Pert 8-Node smart switch is a Wi-Fi enabled smart device, which can easily be installed into your existing 6-8 module switchboard.

RGB light controller

16 million colors to choose from & adjustable color temperature with dimmable light

Multi Sensor

Forget remotes with our Multi Sensor that is a Wi-Fi enabled smart Sensor which can transmit IR waves to control your TV, AC, Music System. Additionally, can detect Temperature, Luminosity & Gas Sensor to keep you aware for any emergencies.
