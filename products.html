Wireless Home Automation

Previous Next

Experience the convenience of wireless home automation with Trublu. we design the automation to cater to your specific needs. With its versatile features, you can easily automate, monitor, schedule, and control all your devices from anywhere at any time.

Take advantage of Scene Control, which allows you to create customized scenes that enable you to control multiple devices and appliances with just a single click, no matter where you are.

Effortlessly schedule the activation and deactivation times for your appliances using the app's Easy Scheduling feature. Whether you need a one-time schedule or a repeated one, you can easily set it up according to your preferences, and the best part is that it works even without an internet connection.

Stay informed with Live Notifications, which provide instant updates whenever the status of any appliance changes or when a scheduled task is executed. This real-time feedback keeps you connected and informed about the activities in your home.With Tru blu wireless home automation, enjoy the benefits of seamless control, enhanced convenience, and increased security, all at your fingertips.
